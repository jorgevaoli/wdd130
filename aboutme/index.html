About Me

Jorge Valverde

Hello everyone! My name is Jorge, and I am from Peru, a country known for having one of the world's finest gastronomies. And yes, of course, I am a big fan of "Pollo a la Brasa," "Lomo Saltado," and "Ceviche." Even though I can prepare these amazing dishes, I tend to be a bit slow in the kitchen, so I prefer to savor and enjoy them.

Another thing I love is reading books, especially physical ones. I'm sorry, but nothing compares to the magic moment of smelling and starting a new book. A couple of years ago, I started to read English books, and this definitely changed my life. Thanks to this I gained self-confidence and finally decided to start my second career, but this time I decided to do it in English. The first career that I studied was International Business Management, and now I have decided to become involved in the growing and exciting world of technology. This is the big reason why I am here, trying to do my best to achieve a bachelor's degree in Software Development.
